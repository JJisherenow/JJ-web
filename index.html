Hi there! I'm John.

I'm a researcher, computer scientist, and mathematician based out of New York. I began my career at a leading tech company, working on novel machine learning techniques and AI research. I later founded a startup focused on applied AI research backed by top investors.

I really like building things–the entire process, from ideation to implementation. I think we have an obligation to leave the world better than we found it, and I believe my best way of doing so is through technological innovation and research. As of late, I've been most interested in deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.

I consider myself a life-long learner–I read textbooks and research papers in my free time. This is not limited to technical subjects; I spend much of my time thinking about social sciences and humanities as well. I'm passionate about effective technical communication and writing; I occasionally post my writing on my blog.

If you'd like to contact me, I can be reached at: john.doe@example.com. I'm always excited to meet other folks doing interesting things in tech.
